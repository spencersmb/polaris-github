We provide customized procedures to treat diverse conditions and illnesses affecting the spine.

At Polaris, we work exclusively with patients experiencing an array of spinal conditions, ranging from mild issues – such as certain disc pains or bone spurs – to more serious conditions – such as spinal stenosis and degenerative disc disease. Our team of experts has decades of experience behind its members and strives tirelessly to stay current and skilled usintg the most up to date diagnostic equipment to pinpoint the root cause of your back pain. Regardless of the condition, we have the ability to locate and treat it as effectively as possible.

The spinal column is one of the most vital parts of the human body, supporting our trunks and making all of our movements possible.
